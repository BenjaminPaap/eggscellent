FAQ

Is there an iPhone or iPad app?
Yes! Cheddar for iOS is free on the App Store and works on iPhone, iPod touch, and iPad. Head over to the apps page for more info.

Is there a Mac app?
Yes! Cheddar for Mac is free on the App Store and runs on OS X Lion or better and requires a 64-bit processor. Head over to the apps page for more info.

What about an app for Android or another platform?
I currently don't have plans for any definite additional platforms, but there is a developer API so you can create your own!

Of course! Here's a full list. I'm working to add more, so if you have any suggestions, please shoot @cheddar a tweet or email support@cheddarapp.com.

How do I delete stuff?
Currently, you can't delete anything. You can archive lists and tasks though. To archive a list, hover over the title, click the pencil to edit, and then click Archive List on the right. The same works for tasks as well. You can also archive lots of tasks at once by clicking Archive Tasks in the top right of your list.

Where are my archived tasks and lists?
Currently, you can't see stuff you've archived. If you need to see some stuff you've archived, email support@cheddarapp.com.
